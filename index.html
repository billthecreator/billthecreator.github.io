Who am I?

Hey there! My name is William Reithmeyer and I'm a year old web designer, programmer, and now an Android Developer.
Click here to see my latest Andriod App -- Tip$
My strongest memory as a child is my curiosity for how things worked. I spent many hours taking things apart and reassembling them. In high school I was introduced to Autoit Script and became interested in computer programming. I began writing programs, designing web pages and working in Photoshop before going away to college.
My college career started in the Engineering department at the University of Pittsburgh. While I did well in engineering courses, I quickly realized that my early passion for computer programming was still very strong. I then made Computer Science the focus of my education.
I earned an Associate of Arts degree in Computer Science in 2011. I later moved and established residency in Florida where I am now finishing my BS in Information System Technology and expect to have that completed this year.
I've worked as a cook most of my college years to support myself. I consider myself a fair golfer and I truly enjoy playing my guitar whenever I get a chance.
