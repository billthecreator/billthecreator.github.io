About us
Founded in 2005, BottomsUp, your exclusive neighborhood beer and wine boutique, proudly sells quality beer and wine from all over the world. With over 25 years of experience our team is passionate about beer and wine and is committed to making sure our customers are satisfied. Special orders are always welcome. BottomsUp has a deep sense of responsibility to our community and our customers.
Our mission
We, at BottomsUp strive to promote and distribute the highest quality of beer and wine. We pride ourselves on our customers and our community. BottomsUp is dedicated to excellence and promises to never compromise quality. We create a fun environment for all of our customers and staff, while also being environmentally friendly and socially responsible.
